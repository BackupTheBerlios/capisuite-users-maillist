Hi,

Am 13.07.2011 15:24, schrieb Marek Gaffke:
> since there was no response on your thread from january 2011, i just want to know if you started to revive capisuite.
>
> I'm interested in that software because i use it for some customers in a productional environment. If you need testers, i have multiple test capabilities thru my job at a german telecom provider.
>
> What is your intention to revive capisuite? New features?

There were approx 10 responses. Seams as if the answers were not send to 
the list. Most said something like: no need any more, ISDN and CAPI will 
die soon. Others said: works, no need for changes. One person had 
enhancement requests, but nobody wanted to help. So I decided not to 
revive capisuite.

I already had started a pure-Python implementation to get rid of the 
C++-core and avoid recompiling if libcapi changes. But since there is no 
need in enhancements, there is even no need in a rewrite :-)
